About Us Yep, we rock !

Our History

A legacy of 25 years and counting, we are in existence since the BASIC, COBOL, Windows 3.1 version days. From Microprocessing days to the days of the internet on web, mobile and gadgets till Internet of Things, we and our products are present, alive and catering the business demands across 9 countries.


We are a software development company specialized in the development of web & mobile based applications, desktop applications and Business Intelligence solutions. Our specialization lies in php, MySQL, Java, .Net, Oracle, SQL Server and other latest technologies. We provide cost effective, efficient and innovative solutions to meet your business needs.


We can be your partner in expanding your business using technology. We help to create vision, strategy and tactical plan to create your presence on the internet. We also design and develop solution for organizational needs. We work with you to harness the latest technology to increase business, reduce cost and open up to new opportunities at a competitive price. Our designs are user friendly, fast to develop and deliver and does speak for itself.
