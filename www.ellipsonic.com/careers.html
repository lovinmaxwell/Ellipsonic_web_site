Software Architects

India

We are looking for an experienced Software Architect to make intuitive high level decisions for software development. You will see the “big picture” and create architectural approaches for software design and implementation to guide the development team.
The goal is to provide a framework for the development of a software or system that will result in high quality IT solutions.

  • Participate in and lead design and code reviews
  • Mentor team members in adopting technologies and tools that you are already familiar with, but which may be new to them
  • Work on infrastructure backlog tasks as assigned
  • Participate in scrum agile development process
  • Perform evaluation and testing of the capabilities, characteristics and requirements of existing and emerging information systems technologies and make recommendations as to feasibility of implementation
  • Design, develop and implement unit and scenario based tests
  • Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication.
  • Assertive: Ability to assert his/her view point in all relevant forums.
  • Leadership: Ability to lead development projects with medium size teams and get the desired results.
  • Passion for technology: Active involvement on various technology communities.
  • Past track record of participation in seminars/forums, keeping updated on evolving technologies, awareness of thought leaders of the industry is a plus point.
